Studio

indie dev, physical focus

this is a small independent studio with one goal: make VR combat feels best while shipping an actual good game.

Approach

Simulation first

Enemies, weapons, and environments are driven by real physical forces, not pre-baked reactions.

Focus

Weight & feedback

Every hit, grab, and recoil is tuned to feel like it's actually happening to something solid.

Scope

Built for VR

Designed around room-scale movement and two hands — not adapted from a flat-screen game.

Stage

Early development

In active development

An untitled VR combat game

Our first title puts you against physically simulated opponents that stagger, block, and recover based on how you actually hit them — not a canned animation. Every weapon has real mass and momentum, and every enemy is built as a ragdoll first, a character second.

  • Full-body physics-based melee and ranged combat
  • Enemies that react to where and how hard you hit them
  • Hands-on interaction with weapons, props, and the environment
  • Built for multiplayer from the ground up
